About Me

I am currently an associate professor at the College of Mathematics and Statistics of Chongqing University. I started my faculty position at ShanghaiTech University as assistant professor (2019.10--2023.03). I performed my academic trainning in the Computational Methods in Systems and Control Theory group, headed by Prof. Dr. Peter Benner at Max Planck Institute for Dynamics of Complex Technical Systems, Magdeburg, Germany.

I obtained my PhD on applied mathematics from the Numerical Analysis group at Delft University of Technology, located in Delft, the Netherlands. I conduct my PhD research under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Martin van Gijzen and Prof. Dr. Kees Vuik. Part of my research work was carried out in the Delft Center for Systems and Control with Prof. Dr. Jan-Willem van Wingerden and Prof. Dr. Michel Verhaegen.

My research interest/focuses are:

  • uncertainty quantification
  • Bayesian inverse problems
  • data assimilation
  • scientific machine learning
